Audrey Hepburn

Audrey Hepburn was a British actress, fashion icon, and humanitarian known for her elegance, grace, and timeless beauty.

She was born on May 4, 1929, in Brussels, Belgium, and passed away on January 20, 1993, in Tolochenaz, Switzerland.
